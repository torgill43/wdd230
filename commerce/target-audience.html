Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to allow visitors, new move-ins, and long-time residents alike to get a feel for what Idaho Falls has to offer, from events to business to restaurants.

Personas:

This site is great for local businesses who are looking to advertise their products/services to those interested or in need.

This site is also a great place for tourists to get a quick peek at what is going on around town.

Scenarios:

  1. A new graduate is looking to see what employment opportunities there are.

  2. A young married couple is looking for dinner options for date night.

  3. A group of friends are looking to see what events are going on.

  4. A startup business is trying to gain traction and wants to advertise to local businesses.

  5. Someone is looking to switch their insurance provider and wants to know what local options there are.
