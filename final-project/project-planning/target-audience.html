Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to display a (fictitious) hotel option for visitors to various national parks throughout the United States (and the world?).

Personas:

This site is a one-stop shop for national park visitors looking for a place to stay.

This site provides a unique opportunity for tourists also looking for an opportunity to offer volunteer service during their visit.

Scenarios:

  1. A tourist or group of tourists looking for a place to stay near a national park.

  2. A tourist or group of tourists looking to offer service.

  3. Guests inquiring about room availabilty.

  4. Guests wondering what the rooms look like.

  5. Guests looking for a contact to inquire about other things to do around the hotel/park.
